About us

GANDHI INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY UNIVERSITY Gunupur,Odisha

Established in 1997, Gandhi Institute of Engineering and Technology is one of the finest Engineering institutions in India. Known for it's highest placement records over the years, it's emerged as the best University in Eastern India. GIET includes various schools such as School of Engineering and Technology, School of Humanities & Social Studies, School of Agriculture,School of Sciences and School of Management Studies. In 2019 it was conferred the rank of University by the Government of Odisha and also approved by UGC. It is also recognized by the Department of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

CIVIL ENGINEERING

Civil engineering is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including public works such as roads, bridges, canals, dams, airports, sewage systems, pipelines, structural components of buildings, and railways.

CHEMICAL ENGINEERING

Chemical engineering is a multi-disciplinary branch of engineering that combines natural and experimental sciences (such as chemistry and physics), along with life sciences (such as biology, microbiology and biochemistry) plus mathematics and economics to design, develop,and produce, transform.
